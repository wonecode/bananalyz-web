<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Can I change my prediction after submitting?">
    Yes — you can update your prediction at any time **before the match starts**.
    Just click the buttons on the match embed again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When do predictions lock?">
    Predictions lock automatically when the match begins.
    The bot syncs match statuses every 5 minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why don't I see any match embeds?">
    Make sure you've run `/config` and selected a predictions channel.
    If matches are still not appearing, check that the bot has **Send Messages** and **Embed Links** permissions in that channel.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I view my stats publicly?">
    Yes — use `/predictions share:True` to post your stats and recent results publicly in the channel.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see someone else's predictions?">
    Yes — use `/predictions user:@username`. Their upcoming picks will be hidden to avoid spoilers 😈
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which leagues are supported?">
    Currently **LEC** is enabled. More leagues are in development.
    You can see the status of all leagues via `/help` on your server.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bot stopped posting match embeds. What do I do?">
    1. Check that the bot is still in your server
    2. Verify it has **Send Messages** + **Embed Links** permissions in the configured channel
    3. Run `/config` again to refresh the channel setting
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
