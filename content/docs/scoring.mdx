Each prediction is worth up to **5 points** (called bananas 🍌).

## Point system

| Outcome | Points |
|---|---|
| ✅ Correct winner | **+3** |
| 🎯 Exact series score | **+2** |
| ✨ Both (perfect prediction) | **+5** |
| ❌ Wrong winner | **+0** |

<Callout type="info" title="Perfect prediction">
  A perfect prediction means you got both the **correct winner** and the **exact series score**.
  That's the maximum 5 bananas in a single match.
</Callout>

## Example

Match: **G2 vs T1**, series format **Bo3**

| Your prediction | Actual result | Points |
|---|---|---|
| G2 wins 2-0 | G2 wins 2-0 | **+5** ✨ perfect |
| G2 wins 2-1 | G2 wins 2-0 | **+3** correct winner |
| T1 wins 2-0 | G2 wins 2-0 | **+0** wrong winner |

## Prediction lock

Predictions are locked automatically when a match starts.
You cannot submit or update your pick after the match has begun.

<Callout type="warn">
  The bot syncs match statuses every **5 minutes**. There may be a short window between kick-off
  and the lock being applied — late submissions during that window may be cancelled.
</Callout>

## Points are final

Once a match result is synced, points are assigned and cannot be changed.
