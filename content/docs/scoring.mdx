## Point system

Each prediction is worth up to **5 points** (called bananas 🍌).

| Outcome | Points |
|---|---|
| Correct winner | **+3** |
| Exact series score | **+2** |
| Both (perfect prediction) | **+5** ✨ |
| Wrong winner | **+0** |

## Example

Match: **G2 vs T1**, series format **Bo3**

| Your prediction | Result | Points |
|---|---|---|
| G2 wins 2-0 | G2 wins 2-0 | **+5** ✨ perfect |
| G2 wins 2-1 | G2 wins 2-0 | **+3** correct winner |
| T1 wins 2-0 | G2 wins 2-0 | **+0** wrong winner |

## Prediction lock

Predictions are locked automatically when a match starts. You cannot submit or update
your pick after the match has begun.

## Points are final

Once a match result is synced, points are assigned and cannot be changed.
The sync runs automatically every 5 minutes.
