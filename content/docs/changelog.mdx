## v1.3.0 — June 2026

**League support & ticker**

- Added league ticker on the landing page showing live supported leagues
- Inactive leagues are now greyed out in the ticker
- Only supported leagues are displayed — removes confusion for unsupported ones

<Callout type="info" title="LEC is live">
    LEC predictions are fully available. LCK and LCS are in progress.
</Callout>

---

## v1.2.0 — May 2026

**Documentation**

- Launched the full documentation site built with Fumadocs
- Added pages: Introduction, Getting Started, Commands, Scoring, FAQ
- Docs are now accessible at `/docs` from the landing page

---

## v1.1.0 — April 2026

**Scoring system**

- Correct winner pick: **+1 point**
- Perfect score prediction: **+3 points**
- Predictions lock automatically when the match starts

<Callout type="warn" title="Predictions lock on match start">
    Once a match begins, predictions are closed. Make sure to submit yours before kick-off.
</Callout>

---

## v1.0.0 — March 2026

**Initial release**

- Bananalyz goes live on Discord
- `/config` to set up the predictions channel
- `/matches` to browse upcoming matches
- `/leaderboard` to view server rankings
- `/predictions` to check your personal stats
- Automatic match sync every 5 minutes
